About Me

Hello! I'm a Ph.D. student at the University of Southern Queensland, where I work on research at the intersection of astronomical instrumentation, CubeSats, and exoplanet science.

My work involves designing and testing space-based instruments, developing small-satellite mission concepts, and exploring new ways to detect and characterize planets beyond our Solar System.
